Michelle Obama

Inspiration is a powerful feeling. It drives people to action. It drives our society into change and growth. People who inspire are leaders, and leaders are valuable to our communities.

Many people know of Michelle Obama as a first lady, but shes also:

  • A published author
  • A motivational speaker
  • An advocate for education, physical activity and healthy eating
  • A role model for many women in the US

The "Obamas" have become icons in the United States. The first lady has been an inspiration and continues to inspire. People like her drive our society into powerful social change.
